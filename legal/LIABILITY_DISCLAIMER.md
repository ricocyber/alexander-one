## CRITICAL SAFETY WARNINGS

### GAS-RELATED WARNINGS

**DANGER - RISK OF EXPLOSION, FIRE, AND DEATH**

- Gas valve installation MUST be performed by a licensed, qualified gas technician
- Only use CSA-certified gas valves rated for your fuel type (natural gas or propane)
- NEVER attempt to install, modify, or repair gas components yourself
- If you smell gas: LEAVE IMMEDIATELY, do not operate any switches, and call 911 from outside
- The ALEXANDER system may FAIL TO DETECT certain gas leaks
- The ALEXANDER system may FAIL TO CLOSE the gas valve in some circumstances
- This system is NOT a substitute for professional gas safety equipment or monitoring

### WATER-RELATED WARNINGS

**WARNING - RISK OF FLOODING AND WATER DAMAGE**

- Water valve installation should be performed by a licensed plumber
- The system may FAIL TO DETECT slow leaks or leaks in certain locations
- The system may FAIL TO CLOSE the water valve in some circumstances
- Valve closure does not drain water already in pipes
- Water damage may still occur even if the system functions correctly
- This system is NOT a substitute for homeowner's insurance

### ELECTRICAL WARNINGS

**WARNING - RISK OF ELECTRICAL SHOCK AND FIRE**

- All electrical connections must comply with local building codes
- Do not install in wet locations without proper enclosures
- Use only the power supply provided
- Do not open the control hub or modify any components
- Disconnect power before performing any maintenance

### FIRE AND SMOKE WARNINGS

**WARNING - RISK OF FIRE, INJURY, AND DEATH**

- This system is NOT a substitute for code-required smoke detectors
- Smoke sensors may not detect all types of fires
- Smoke sensors may not detect fires in all locations
- The stove safety feature may FAIL in some circumstances
- Always supervise cooking and never leave cooking unattended
