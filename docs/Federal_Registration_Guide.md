# 1. SAM.gov REGISTRATION (Do First - Required for Everything)

## What is SAM.gov?
System for Award Management - required for ALL federal grants and contracts.

## Time Required
- Initial registration: 30-45 minutes
- Activation: 7-10 business days (can be faster)

## Before You Start - Gather These:

| Document | Where to Find |
|----------|---------------|
| D-U-N-S Number | You have this ✓ |
| EIN (Tax ID) | IRS letter |
| Bank account info | Cielo Azul LLC business account |
| Bank routing number | On checks or bank statement |
| CAGE Code | Will be assigned during registration |

## Step-by-Step Registration:

### Step 1: Go to SAM.gov
- URL: https://sam.gov
- Click "Sign In" (top right)
- Click "Create an Account" if you don't have Login.gov account

### Step 2: Create Login.gov Account
- Use your personal email
- Set up 2-factor authentication (required)
- Verify your email

### Step 3: Start Entity Registration
- After login, click "Get Started" under "Register Your Entity"
- Select "I want to register my entity to do business with the federal government"

### Step 4: Enter Core Data
```
Legal Business Name: Cielo Azul LLC
Doing Business As (DBA): [Leave blank or add I.H.P. if you want]
Physical Address: 4801 N Blackwelder Ave, Oklahoma City, OK 73118
Mailing Address: [Same as above]
Start Date: [Date LLC was formed]
Fiscal Year End: December 31
```

### Step 5: Enter D-U-N-S
- Enter your D-U-N-S number
- System will validate it (may take a few minutes)

### Step 6: Enter NAICS Codes
NAICS codes describe your business type. Use these:

| Code | Description | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| 541715 | Research and Development in Physical Sciences | I.H.P. R&D |
| 334290 | Other Communications Equipment Manufacturing | Sensor hardware |
| 541512 | Computer Systems Design Services | Software platform |
| 236220 | Commercial and Institutional Building Construction | LUXX BUILDZ |
| 531390 | Other Real Estate Activities | Real estate holdings |

Primary NAICS: **541715** (R&D - this is what SBIR wants to see)

### Step 7: Enter SBA Size Standards
- Number of employees: 1-5
- Annual revenue: Under $1 million
- Check "Small Business" - YES

### Step 8: Banking Information
```
Bank Name: [Your bank]
Account Type: Checking
Routing Number: [9-digit routing]
Account Number: [Your account number]
```
This is for receiving federal payments via ACH.

### Step 9: Points of Contact

**Government Business POC:**
```
Name: Eric De Loera
Title: Owner/Principal
Phone: [Your phone]
Email: [Your email]
```

**Electronic Business POC:** (Can be same person)
```
Name: Eric De Loera
Title: Owner/Principal
Phone: [Your phone]
Email: [Your email]
```

### Step 10: Representations and Certifications

Check YES for these if they apply:
- [ ] Small Business
- [ ] Small Disadvantaged Business (Hispanic-owned qualifies)
- [ ] Minority-Owned Business
- [ ] Self-Certified Small Disadvantaged Business

Check NO for:
- Debarment/suspension questions
- Criminal conviction questions
- Tax delinquency questions

### Step 11: Review and Submit
- Review all information
- Click Submit
- Save your confirmation number

### Step 12: Wait for Activation
- Takes 7-10 business days
- You'll receive email confirmation
- CAGE code will be assigned automatically

# 3. GRANTS.gov REGISTRATION

## What is Grants.gov?
Central portal for ALL federal grant opportunities (not just SBIR).

## Time Required: 15 minutes

## Prerequisites:
- SAM.gov registration must be ACTIVE first
- You need your SAM.gov UEI number (assigned during SAM registration)

## Step-by-Step:

### Step 1: Go to Grants.gov
- URL: https://www.grants.gov
- Click "Register" (top right)

### Step 2: Choose Registration Type
- Select "Organization Applicant"
- You're registering on behalf of Cielo Azul LLC

### Step 3: Link to SAM.gov
- Enter your UEI (Unique Entity ID from SAM.gov)
- System will pull your organization info from SAM.gov

### Step 4: Create Authorized Organization Representative (AOR)
```
Name: Eric De Loera
Title: Owner/Principal Investigator
Email: [Your email]
Phone: [Your phone]
```

### Step 5: E-Business Point of Contact Approval
- If you're the only person, you approve yourself
- This lets you submit grant applications

### Step 6: Set Up Workspace
- Create "Workspaces" for grant applications you're working on
- You can invite team members later

### Step 7: Subscribe to Opportunities
Search for and subscribe to:
- "Smart Home" grants
- "Energy Efficiency" grants
- "IoT" grants
- "Small Business Innovation" grants

# TROUBLESHOOTING

## SAM.gov Issues:
- **D-U-N-S not found:** Call D&B at 1-866-705-5711
- **Bank validation failed:** Double-check routing/account numbers
- **Taking too long:** Call SAM help desk 1-866-606-8220

## SBIR.gov Issues:
- **Can't find opportunities:** Use filters (Agency, Topic, Open Date)
- **Account locked:** Reset password via email

## Grants.gov Issues:
- **UEI not recognized:** SAM.gov registration not active yet
- **Can't submit:** Need AOR approval first
