# SECTION 2: COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

## Flo by Moen (Water)
- **Claim Reduction:** 96% reduction in water claims (LexisNexis study)
- **Severity Reduction:** 72% decrease in claim severity
- **Study Size:** 2,306 homes analyzed
- **Key Stat:** Within 30 days of install, 60% of homeowners notified of unknown leak
- **Partnerships:** Amica, Liberty Mutual, Farmers Insurance
- **Source:** [LexisNexis Press Release](https://risk.lexisnexis.com/about-us/press-room/press-release/20200505-flo-by-moen)

## Ting / Whisker Labs (Electrical)
- **Homes Protected:** 900,000+ sensors installed
- **Fire Prevention:** 80%+ of electrical fires predicted and prevented
- **Hazard Detection:** 1 in 60 homes find fire/water hazard annually
- **Growth Rate:** 50,000 new homes per month
- **Claims Reduction:** $81 annual fire claims reduction per home by year 3
- **Study:** 1.1 million home-years of experience, 433 fire claims analyzed
- **Insurance Partners:** Nationwide (50,000+ homes), State Farm, SageSure, Westfield, Farm Bureau Michigan
- **Source:** [Whisker Labs](https://www.whiskerlabs.com/insurance/), [Nationwide Press Release](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/nationwide-reaches-50-000-home-milestone-with-whisker-labs-ting-program-reducing-fire-and-water-loss-risk-302117207.html)

## LeakBot / Ondo InsurTech (Water)
- **Claim Cost Reduction:** 70% reduction in water damage claim costs
- **Homes Deployed:** 151,000+ globally
- **Insurance Partners:** 24 carriers globally
- **NPS Score:** +87 (pilot program)
- **Customer Satisfaction:** 4.9/5
- **Monthly Fee to Insurers:** $5/month per device (US)
- **Major Partner:** Nationwide (expanded to 16 states, Nov 2024)
- **Source:** [Ondo InsurTech](https://www.ondoplc.com/), [Insurance Edge](https://insurance-edge.net/2024/11/12/leakbot-rolled-out-to-16-more-states-in-us-market/)

## Phyn (Water)
- **Claim Reduction:** 99% less likely to experience non-weather water leak claim
- **Study Size:** 13,500 homes over 3 years
- **Industry Impact:** Reducing nearly $9B in annual insurance claims
- **Insurance Partners:** Nationwide (15% discount), PURE, VYRD, AIG, Amica, Chubb
- **Advantage:** More insurance partnerships than competitors
- **Source:** [Phyn](https://phyn.com/), [Nationwide Partnership](https://news.nationwide.com/nationwide-and-phyn-partner-to-offer-advanced-water-damageprotection-for-homeowners/)
