# PHASE 2: STARTER TRADELINES (Week 2-4)

## What are Tradelines?

Tradelines are vendor accounts that report your payment history to business credit bureaus. Pay them on time (or early) to build your Paydex score.

## Net-30 Accounts (No Personal Credit Check)

Apply for these in order. Each approval builds credibility for the next.

### Tier 1: Easy Approval (Apply First)

| Vendor | What They Sell | Reports To | Min Order | Website |
|--------|---------------|------------|-----------|---------|

| **Quill** | Office supplies | D&B | ~$50 | quill.com |
| **Grainger** | Industrial supplies | D&B | ~$50 | grainger.com |

**How to Apply:**
1. Go to vendor website
2. Click "Apply for Credit" or "Business Account"
3. Enter business info (EIN, D-U-N-S, address)
4. Request Net-30 terms
5. Wait for approval (usually 1-5 days)
6. Make a purchase

# TRACKING YOUR PROGRESS

## Paydex Score (Dun & Bradstreet)

| Score | Rating | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 80-100 | Low Risk | Pay early or on time |
| 50-79 | Medium Risk | Some late payments |
| 0-49 | High Risk | Significant late payments |

**Target:** 80+ (pay all invoices EARLY)

## How Paydex is Calculated

| Payment Timing | Points |
|----------------|--------|
| 30+ days early | 100 |
| 20-30 days early | 90 |
| On time | 80 |
| 1-14 days late | 70 |
| 15-30 days late | 60 |
| 31-60 days late | 50 |
| 61-90 days late | 40 |
| 91+ days late | 20 |

**Strategy:** Pay ALL invoices at least 20 days early to maximize score.

# CRITICAL RULES

## DO:
- ✓ Pay EARLY (20+ days before due date)
- ✓ Use each account at least once per month
- ✓ Keep utilization under 30%
- ✓ Monitor your D&B profile monthly
- ✓ Keep business and personal separate
- ✓ Use business bank account for all payments

## DON'T:
- ✗ Apply for personal credit cards "for business"
- ✗ Give personal SSN unless absolutely required
- ✗ Miss any payment deadlines
- ✗ Max out credit lines
- ✗ Close old accounts (length of history matters)
- ✗ Apply for too many accounts at once (spread out)
