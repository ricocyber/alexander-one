# SECTION 1: FEDERAL SBIR/STTR GRANTS (Best Fit for I.H.P.)

## Overview
- **Non-dilutive funding** (you keep 100% equity)
- **Phase I:** Up to $314,363
- **Phase II:** Up to $2,095,748
- **11 federal agencies** participate

## Key Agencies for I.H.P. Smart Home Tech

| Agency | Focus Area | I.H.P. Fit | Deadline Window |
|--------|------------|-----------|-----------------|
| **DOE (Dept of Energy)** | Energy efficiency, smart grid | Tesla integration, HVAC monitoring | May-Aug, Sept-Jan |
| **NSF** | Technology innovation | IoT sensors, Matter protocol | Jan-Apr, Jun-Aug |
| **DHS (Homeland Security)** | Home security, resilience | Fire/water prevention | Sept-Oct 2025 |
| **HHS** | Health & safety | Environmental sensors, CO detection | Apr-Nov |
| **USDA** | Rural technology | Rural home protection | Jun-Aug |

## Application Website
**SBIR.gov** - All opportunities listed here

## Eligibility Requirements
- U.S. small business (under 500 employees)
- For-profit company
- 51%+ owned by U.S. citizens
- Principal researcher primarily employed by company

## I.H.P. SBIR Pitch Angles
1. **DOE:** Energy monitoring reduces grid strain, integrates with Tesla Powerwall
2. **DHS:** Home fire/water prevention = homeland resilience
3. **NSF:** Multi-peril IoT correlation = novel technology innovation
4. **HHS:** Environmental sensors (CO, air quality) = public health
